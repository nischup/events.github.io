The Best Brand Loyalty is Built with
iGo Events and Mobile Apps

What’s next after the event? How do you handle the credit balance? How can I continue to engage the customers? Is retargeting possible by promoting other events to the same customers? Do these questions sound familiar? While others thought of pre and actual event supports, iGo goes beyond and above. We believe in relationship, engagement, and trust. Hence, iGo Events and iGo Mobile Apps were launched to continue engaging with the customers as part of iGo Ecosystem for post-events supports. Customers with credit balance can continue to enjoy using the balance for other event booking, retail shopping and F&B promotions in iGo Events site and eWallet Apps. Check out iGo Events or simply download iGo eWallet Apps to enjoy what we have to offer.
